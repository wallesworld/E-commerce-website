FAQ

Are AEVORA products vegan & cruelty free?

Yes! All our products are 100% vegan and we never test on animals. We are committed to ethical practises from ingredients sourcing to the final bottle.

Are your prdoucts suitable for sensitive skin?

Our formulas are designed wuth minimalism in mind, using only high-quality, cold-prressed oils and natural botanicals. However, we always recommend doing a patch test on a small area of skin before your first full application.

My favourite product is sold out! When can I buy it?

I want to return an item.
